Features

Simple Setup

VOMO’s easy to use platform allows for quick and easy event set up.

Create & Manage Events

VOMO simplifies and automates the entire process start to finish.

Privacy Settings

Events can be privately held or open to the public.  Your organization decides how to structure each project.

Discover local causes

Don’t know where to begin?  VOMO’s location-based platform can help you find opportunities near you or your organization.

Show & Share Impact

Create ready-to-share reports at end of your volunteer project .

Generate Detailed Reports

See your community impact with comprehensive reports, including the total value of service, down to the dollar.

Check-in Capabilities

No need for pen and paper to track your volunteers.  They simply check-in upon arrival with a click of the VOMO app.

Social Integration

Share the good news of your volunteer movement with friends and family with seamless social integration.
